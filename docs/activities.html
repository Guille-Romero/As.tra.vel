Surfing Winds

Venus -Clouds-

Do you want to surf... in the air???!!!

A phenomenon called super-rotation produces perpetual winds on Venus, and the atmosphere circles the planet in just over four Earth days. At the tops of Venus' clouds, winds have been measured as high as 224 mph (360 kph)!
